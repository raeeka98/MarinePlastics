How to : Update Survey
1. Add new updates in appropriate subsection file of the survey found in
   MarinePlastics/client/src/NewSurveyForm/SurveySubsections
2. If you are adding an entire new field, add variable in
   MarinePlastics/client/src/NewSurveyForm/SurveyForm.jsx in validate()
3. Update MarinePlastics/client/src/NewSurveyForm/SurveySubsections/Review.jsx
